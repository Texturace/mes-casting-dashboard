FAQ

What is a machine activation?

Each PC you install MES Dashboard on uses one activation. Solo plans allow 1 machine, Org plans allow up to 8. You can deactivate old machines by contacting support.

What happens when my subscription expires?

You get a 3-day grace period before the app requires renewal. Your data is never deleted.

How do I receive my license key?

Your license key is emailed to you automatically after purchase. Enter it in the app's activation screen to get started.

Can I upgrade from Solo to Org?

Yes — contact support and we'll help you switch plans and transfer your remaining time.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day demo so you can try before committing. Refunds are handled case by case — contact support.
